Prague, often referred to as the "City of a Hundred Spires," is one of Europe's most enchanting cities, renowned for its medieval charm, stunning architecture, and vibrant cultural scene. The city’s Old Town (Staré Město) is a UNESCO World Heritage site, featuring cobblestone streets, historic buildings, and iconic landmarks such as the Astronomical Clock, the Charles Bridge, and the Gothic-style Prague Castle. Prague’s architectural landscape is a beautiful mix of styles, including Gothic, Baroque, and Romanesque, with breathtaking structures scattered throughout the city. The Prague Castle, perched on a hill overlooking the city, is the largest ancient castle in the world and offers panoramic views of Prague’s stunning skyline. The Charles Bridge, adorned with statues of saints, is another must-see attraction, connecting the Old Town with the Lesser Quarter.
